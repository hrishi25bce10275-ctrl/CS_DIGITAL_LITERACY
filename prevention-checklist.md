# Stay Safe Online - College Student Checklist (India)

## Prevention Tips

### General Online Safety
- **Use strong, unique passwords** (12+ characters, mix of letters/numbers/symbols) for each app/website
- **Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on email, UPI apps, college portals wherever available
- **Avoid public Wi-Fi** for banking/shopping; use mobile hotspot or college secure networks only
- **Never click links** in unsolicited WhatsApp messages, emails, or SMS claiming "account issues"

### UPI/Financial Safety (Mandatory)
- **Check UPI ID before sending money** - scammers use fake "family member" IDs like familyname@paytm
- **Set UPI transaction limits** (₹5,000/day max) and enable "Allow only specific amounts" in BHIM/PhonePe
- **Verify beneficiary name** matches before every UPI transaction (scammers use similar names)

### Social Media & Academic Safety
- **Don't share semester/exam details** publicly - helps scammers time phishing attacks
- **Screenshot fake job/internship offers** before responding - verify company domain first
